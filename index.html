HEXA

Objective

The objective of the game of Hexa is to score as many points as you can by rolling 'scoring' dice

Scoring

Begin by rolling all six dice and check to see if you have rolled any scoring dice or combinations. Any dice that score will be set aside and then you may choose to roll all the remaining dice.


If all six dice have been set aside for scoring, you can choose to roll all six dice again and continue adding to your accumulated score or you can save your points.


Your turn continues until either you decide to stop or until you fail to roll any scoring dice, which will result in you loosing your accumulated score for that round.
